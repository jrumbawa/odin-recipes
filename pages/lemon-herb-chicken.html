Lemon Herb Chicken & Vegies

Description

An easy, wholesome meal where tender chicken is baked alongside baby potatoes and green beans. Infused with lemon, garlic, and herbs, it’s a complete, fuss-free dinner that’s both healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 cups baby potatoes
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss potatoes and green beans with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken on a baking sheet and arrange veggies around it. Add lemon slices on top.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and veggies are tender.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
