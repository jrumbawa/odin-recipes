Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

A quick and flavorful dish featuring succulent shrimp cooked in a rich garlic butter sauce. Perfectly seasoned with paprika and garnished with fresh parsley, it’s great served with rice, pasta, or crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 3 tsbp Butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and 2 tbsp butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and opaque.
  4. Stir in the remaining butter and garnish with parsley.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges and enjoy with rice or crusty bread.
