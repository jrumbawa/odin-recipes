Caprese Salad

Description

A classic Italian salad made with fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. Drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, it’s a light, refreshing, and colorful dish that’s perfect as a starter or side.

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating them.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately as a light and refreshing dish.
